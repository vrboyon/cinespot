Vikram: Hitlist" is an Indian action thriller film released in 2022, directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The film stars Kamal Haasan in the titular role, supported by Vijay Sethupathi and Fahadh Faasil. The plot revolves around a retired RAW agent, Vikram, who is drawn back into the shadows to eliminate a notorious drug syndicate. Renowned for its gripping storyline, intense action sequences, and stellar performances, "Vikram: Hitlist" also features a notable score by Anirudh Ravichander. The movie combines high-octane action with a compelling narrative, making it a standout entry in contemporary Indian cinema.

"Kaithi" is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language action thriller directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The film stars Karthi as Dilli, an ex-convict who, after being released from prison, is desperate to meet his daughter for the first time. However, his plans are thwarted when he is roped into helping a group of policemen, led by Narain's character, to battle a dangerous drug cartel. Set over a single night, "Kaithi" is lauded for its taut screenplay, intense action, and Karthi's powerful performance. The film's unique narrative structure and gritty realism have made it a critical and commercial success..

"Mahaan" is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action-drama film directed by Karthik Subbaraj. The film stars Vikram in the lead role, alongside his son Dhruv Vikram, marking their first on-screen collaboration. "Mahaan" follows the story of Gandhi Mahaan, a man who breaks away from his family's Gandhian principles to embrace a life of crime and luxury. The narrative delves into themes of freedom, rebellion, and the consequences of one's choices. With powerful performances, especially by Vikram and Dhruv, the film is noted for its engaging storyline, stylish direction, and a captivating score by Santhosh Narayanan.

"Master" is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action-thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The film stars Thalapathy Vijay as JD, an alcoholic professor sent to a juvenile school, where he clashes with Bhavani, played by Vijay Sethupathi, a ruthless gangster exploiting the children. The film explores themes of redemption and the battle between good and evil. With its intense action sequences, gripping narrative, and powerful performances, especially by the two Vijays, "Master" became a commercial success. The film's music, composed by Anirudh Ravichander, further amplifies its dynamic energy, making "Master" a standout in contemporary Tamil cinema.

"The Shawshank Redemption," directed by Frank Darabont and released in 1994, is a profound American drama based on Stephen King's novella. The film stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, and Morgan Freeman as Red, a long-term inmate. Set in the oppressive Shawshank prison, the story chronicles Andy's resilience, hope, and friendship with Red over two decades. The film explores themes of justice, redemption, and the human spirit's endurance. Despite initial box office struggles, it garnered critical acclaim and became a beloved classic, celebrated for its compelling performances, storytelling, and emotional depth.

"Leo" is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The film stars Thalapathy Vijay in the titular role, portraying a seemingly ordinary man with a hidden past that comes to light amidst a series of violent events. The narrative unfolds with intense action sequences, suspenseful twists, and a deeper exploration of the protagonist's complex character. With a supporting cast including Trisha and Sanjay Dutt, the film is noted for its high-octane action, engaging screenplay, and powerful performances. Anirudh Ravichander’s music and Kanagaraj's direction contribute to making "Leo" a significant addition to contemporary Tamil cinema.
